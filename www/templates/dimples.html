DISAPPEARING DIMPLE TRAITS    

If no one has ever had dimples in your family, you probably won't either, because the combination of genes needed to produce this trait doesn't run in your family.

If you do have dimples, but they disappeared after childhood, one of your parents probably also had dimples that disappeared with age.

If you have dimples and one of your parents still has dimples, they your probably won't disappear either. close
